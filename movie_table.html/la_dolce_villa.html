LA DOLCE VILLA

La Dolce Villa is a 2025 American romantic comedy film directed by Mark Waters. It was released on Netflix on February 13, 2025.

Premise

After his daughter buys a cheap Italian villa to restore, Eric travels to Italy to intervene.
